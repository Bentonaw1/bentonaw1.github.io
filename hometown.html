Edgecomb, Maine Hometown of the most important man in the universe.

Edgecomb, Maine is located in the Boothbay Region of Midcoast, Maine. Guarding its populace, most of which are aged over 50 years, is the grandstanding Fort Edgecomb. Though its blockhouse seems meager standing, its battlement's remains are visible along its shoreline.

Other than Fort Edgecomb, notable places include Edgecomb Elementary School, The Edgecomb Town Hall, Fire Department, and Edgecomb Potters.
